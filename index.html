How to play?

Instructions

  1. Pick your weapon of choice: Instagram or Twitter.
  2. Find #hermanskilegend & take photo.
  3. Use #hashtags to enter the photostream or twitter feed to claim your reward.
  4. Take the throne on the scoreboard and win great prices.
  5. Profit!

#Hashtag + #Xhp

Use as many hashtags as your conscience allows, but you MUST use the #hermanskilegend hashtag:

  • #hermanskilegend

...and include your claim for glory, aka Herman points (#hp), to get on the scoreboard.

Your questions & our answers

What happens when I cheat?

All your points will be removed. Yeah, that's the way the Herminator crumbles.

What's in the price pool?

You'll see on monday lunch. You won't be disappointed.

Why are you doing this?

We all know and love Her-man. He(r)man (awesome dude who rides a tiger who wears a helmet!). Herminator. A true legend. On skis!

My Instagram photos doesn't show up!

You have to make sure that your Instagram profile is public, otherwise we can't sniff it. Make it public for the weekend, you can always switch back right after. Or use Twitter instead. Also, make sure that you've used the correct hashtag, #hermanskilegend. That's all, back to the slopes with ya!

When is the Herminators birthday?

On sunday, March 29th. His greatest wish is that you give him a hug and a beer. And of course that you make the moment last forever by capturing it on film and publish it on Instagram or Twitter. Sharing is caring!

What happened to the selfie-ception quest for 50 hp?

It's now only worth 10 hp. It's a supply and demand thing.
